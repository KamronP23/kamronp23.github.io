Therapy for Men Navigating Stress, Anxiety & Relationships

Work pressure, financial stress, anxiety, and relationship challenges can pile up fast. Therapy can help you get clarity, reduce stress, and build practical tools to move forward.

Online therapy for men in California & Florida • Licensed Therapist

This page is specifically for men dealing with anxiety, work or financial stress, burnout, and relationship challenges.

A lot of adults come to therapy not because things are “falling apart,” but because life feels heavy — work stress is relentless, relationships are complicated, motivation is low, or the pressure to keep up is constant. Sometimes it shows up as anxiety, irritability, shutdown, overthinking, or feeling stuck.

I offer virtual individual therapy for adults in California and Florida. My style is collaborative, grounded, and practical — we’ll make space to understand what’s happening while also building tools you can use day to day.

Common reasons people reach out:

  • • Chronic stress, pressure, or feeling “on” all the time
  • • Burnout, work overload, or difficulty setting boundaries
  • • Anxiety, overthinking, or trouble sleeping
  • • Low mood, disconnection, or feeling stuck
  • • Relationship strain, dating challenges, or communication issues
  • • Major life transitions or career changes

Working with Men

Many of the clients I work with are men navigating anxiety, burnout, relationships, dating, career pressure, and identity shifts. Therapy doesn’t have to feel clinical or intimidating — we keep it practical, straightforward, and focused on real-life stressors.

All services are provided via secure telehealth platforms. I work with adults located in California and Florida.
